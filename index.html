About Tashkent Supermarket

Tashkent Supermarket brings the finest halal meats, fresh produce, and specialty foods from Central Asia and beyond to the heart of Manhattan. Our knowledgeable staff and commitment to quality ensure you'll find authentic ingredients for traditional recipes alongside everyday essentials, all certified halal and competitively priced.

20 Years of Excellence

Our Services

Fresh Halal Meat & Poultry Butcher Counter

Expert butchers providing custom cuts of premium halal-certified meats and poultry, sourced from trusted suppliers.

Central Asian & Middle Eastern Specialty Foods

Authentic imported products including spices, dried fruits, nuts, and traditional ingredients from Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, and beyond.

Fresh Produce & Organic Selection

Daily deliveries of fresh fruits and vegetables, including hard-to-find Central Asian varieties and organic options.

Freshly Baked Tandoor Bread & Pastries

Traditional breads and pastries baked fresh daily in our authentic tandoor oven, including nan, somsa, and more.

Custom Spice Blending & Bulk Dry Goods

Create your perfect spice mix with our extensive selection, plus bulk grains, legumes, and dried goods at competitive prices.
